Read Stories Base on your Level & Interest

Change Stories to Your Level and Region

Each story has four levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advance, and Native. The vocabulary, grammar and overall style of writing, changes to fit each respective level.

Listen to Stories in different regional accents

Each story has a listening track. The default track uses the Standard for the respective language. You also can choose from varies accents for each language. For example, Spanish would have the following accents: Spain, Mexico, Cuba, Peru, etc.

Convert stories to learning paths

A learning path includes fun activities to reinforce the vocabulary, grammar and sentence patterns that appears in each respective story.

Languages & Dialects

Our List of Languages Extends Globally

Indo-European languages

The Indo-European language family is one of the world's largest and most widespread language families, encompassing a diverse range of languages spoken across Europe, the Americas, South Asia, and parts of the Middle East.

Sino-Tibetan Languages

The Sino-Tibetan language family is one of the world's largest language families, encompassing a wide range of languages spoken across East Asia, Southeast Asia, and parts of South Asia.

Afroasiatic Languages

The Afroasiatic language family is one of the oldest and most widespread language families, primarily spoken across North Africa, the Horn of Africa, and parts of the Middle East.

Austronesian language family

The Austronesian language family is one of the world's largest language families, encompassing a vast array of languages spoken across Southeast Asia, the Pacific Islands, and parts of Madagascar.
