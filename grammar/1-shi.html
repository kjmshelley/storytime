Word List

I am a student. New York is a city. This is a car. They were friends.
我 是 学生。 纽约是城市。 这是车。 他们是朋友。
Wǒ shì xuésheng. Niǔyuē shì chéngshì. Zhè shì chē. Tāmen shì péngyǒu.
Wǒ = xuésheng. Niǔyuē = chéngshì. Zhè = chē. Tāmen = péngyǒu.

Sentence Pattern

Subject

[Person]/[Thing]/[Place]/[Activity]/[Idea]

Verb

是 (shì)

Object

[Person]/[Thing]/[Place]/[Activity]/[Idea]

是 (shì) can be loosely translated as to be, am, is, was, were.

But it's function can be though of as =, it links the subject and object.

For example,

I am a student. New York is a city. This is a car. They were friends.
我 是 学生。 纽约是城市。 这是车。 他们是朋友。
Wǒ shì xuésheng. Niǔyuē shì chéngshì. Zhè shì chē. Tāmen shì péngyǒu.
Wǒ = xuésheng. Niǔyuē = chéngshì. Zhè = chē. Tāmen = péngyǒu.
